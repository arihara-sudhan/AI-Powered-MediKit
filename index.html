Heart

CARDIOMEGALY: Enlargement of the heart.

Treatment: Medications, surgery, lifestyle changes.

NORMAL: A healthy heart without abnormalities.

Brain

GLIOMA: A type of tumor that occurs in the brain and spinal cord.

Treatment: Surgery, radiation therapy, chemotherapy.

MENINGIOMA: A tumor that forms on membranes covering the brain and spinal cord.

Treatment: Surgery, radiation therapy.

NORMAL: Healthy brain tissue without abnormalities.

PITUITARY TUMOR: A growth in the pituitary gland at the base of the brain.

Treatment: Surgery, radiation therapy, medication.

Bone

FRACTURED: A break or crack in the bone.

Treatment: Casting, surgery, physical therapy.

NOT FRACTURED: Healthy bone without fractures.

Breast

CANCER: Breast cancer occurs when malignant cells form in the tissues of the breast.

Treatment: Surgery, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, hormone therapy, targeted therapy.

NORMAL: Healthy breast tissue without abnormalities.

Eye

ACRIMA: Conditions associated with dry eye or lacrimal gland issues.

Treatment: Artificial tears, medications, lifestyle changes, surgery.

ODIR: Dataset-related condition; specifics unknown.

ORIGA: Related to detecting glaucoma.

Treatment: Medications, laser therapy, surgery.

GLAUCOMA: A condition damaging the optic nerve due to high intraocular pressure.

Treatment: Eye drops, medications, laser treatment, surgery.

CATARACT: Clouding of the eye's lens leading to decreased vision.

Treatment: Surgery to replace the lens.

RETINAL DISEASE: Includes various conditions like diabetic retinopathy and macular degeneration.

Treatment: Medications, laser therapy, surgery.

Colon

LESION: Abnormal tissue found in the colon, potentially benign or precancerous.

Treatment: Removal via colonoscopy, monitoring, surgery.

NORMAL: Healthy colon tissue without abnormalities.

ECG (Electrocardiogram)

NORMAL: A standard ECG reading with no abnormalities.

VENTRICULAR: Ventricular arrhythmias, abnormal heart rhythms in the lower chambers.

Treatment: Medications, ablation, implantable devices, surgery.

UNKNOWN: Unspecified or unclear ECG result.

Treatment: Further diagnostic testing.

SUPRAVENTRICULAR: Arrhythmias originating above the ventricles.

Treatment: Medications, cardioversion, ablation, surgery.

FUSION: Combination of normal and abnormal heart impulses.

Treatment: Depends on underlying cause.

Kidney

CYST: Fluid-filled sacs in the kidneys, usually benign.

Treatment: Monitoring, draining, surgery.

NORMAL: Healthy kidney tissue.

STONE: Hard deposits in the kidneys.

Treatment: Increased water intake, medications, lithotripsy, surgery.

TUMOR: Abnormal growths in the kidneys, benign or malignant.

Treatment: Surgery, chemotherapy, radiation, targeted therapy.

Lungs

PNEUMONIA (BACTERIAL): Lung infection caused by bacteria.

Treatment: Antibiotics, rest, fluids.

CORONA: COVID-19 infection.

Treatment: Symptomatic treatment, antivirals, oxygen therapy.

NORMAL: Healthy lung tissue.

TUBERCULOSIS: Bacterial infection of the lungs.

Treatment: Multiple antibiotics over a long period.

PNEUMONIA (VIRAL): Lung infection caused by viruses.

Treatment: Rest, fluids, antivirals, supportive care.

Blood

BENIGN: Non-cancerous blood conditions.

Treatment: Monitoring, treatment based on the condition.

MALIGNANT (PRE-B): Precursor B-cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia (ALL).

Treatment: Chemotherapy, targeted therapy, radiation, stem cell transplant.

MALIGNANT (PRO B): B-cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia (ALL).

Treatment: Similar to PRE-B; chemotherapy, targeted therapy, stem cell transplant.

MALIGNANT (EARLY PRE-B): Early stage B-cell ALL.

Treatment: Chemotherapy, targeted therapy, stem cell transplant.

Oral

NORMAL: Healthy oral tissue.

CANCER: Malignant cells in the oral cavity.

Treatment: Surgery, radiation therapy, chemotherapy.
